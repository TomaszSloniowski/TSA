Single-family house

The concept assumes the reconstruction of a single-family house from the 1970s into two single-family semi-detached houses. The underground part was adapted to the garage, while the ground floor underwent a complete metamorphosis. The two twin, two-segment parts are visually one cohesive whole, fastened by the slightly elevated floor, emphasized by a wooden cladding on the façade. Apartments on the first floor also have large terraces with pergolas at the roof level. The wider shape of the building from the front side has staircases located on the sides, nabling independent access to apartments.
